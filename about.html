Thanks for stopping by!

Design found me at the intersection of two things I loved: making beautiful, well thought out things that solved problems that mattered to real people. Five years in, that's still what gets me out of bed.

I'm a Senior Product Designer at Meta, where I've spent my career working across some of the most complex and foundational infrastructure in ads and commerce. I turn fragmented, technically complex problem spaces into clear product direction, and stay close to the details, the systems, and the people so nothing important gets lost. I craft with care, and build with teams, not for them.

When I'm not designing, you can find me outdoors, eating my way through a new city, making very tiny fruits, or on the hunt for a good coffee shop.
